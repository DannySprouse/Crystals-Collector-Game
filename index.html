How to Play Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. It will be displayed below under Your Target Total.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to Your Current Total.

You win the game by clicking on the crystals to get Your Current Total to exactly match Your Target Total. You lose the game if Your Current Total goes above the Your Target Total number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Once clicked, that amount is added to your current total. Pay attention to the value of each crystal you click as you might need to click that amount again!

You can click on the same crystal multiple times if you wish - just be careful to not go over the Your Target Total number!

Each time when the game re-starts, the game will change the values of each crystal, will reset Your Current Total back to 0, and a new Your Target Total number will be set for you to meet. Your Wins and Losses will be calculated for you.
